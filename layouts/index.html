The Australian Text Analytics Platform is an open source environment that provides researchers with tools and training for analysing, processing, and exploring text.




The Language Data Commons of Australia (LDaCA) and Australian Text Analytics Platform (ATAP) projects received investment (https://doi.org/10.47486/DP768 and https://doi.org/10.47486/PL074) from the Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC). The ARDC is funded by the National Collaborative Research Infrastructure Strategy (NCRIS).
